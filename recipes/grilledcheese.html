Grilled Cheese

Description

We love cheese. Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich here.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slice Bread
  • Cheese(s) of choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grab the bread slices and set them down flat.
  2. Place cheese(s) on them and combine together with the cheese in the middle.
  3. Heat pan/skiller on medium-low heat and place 2 tbsp of butter in the pan.
  4. Once pan is hot and the butter is melted, place the sandwich on it.
  5. Toast the sandwich until the cheese is all melted and both sides are browned and crispy.
  6. Let cool slightly and enjoy!
