Guacamole

Description

We love avocados. Learn how to make guacamole here.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Avocado
  • Red Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt
  • (Optional) Herbs of Choice (e.g. cilantro)

Steps

  1. Remove avocado flesh and mash into a creamy consistency.
  2. Dice the tomato and red onion and add to the mashed avocado.
  3. Add a hefty dash of lime juice for flavor (and to preserve color).
  4. Add a pinch of salt and the herbs if desired, and mix.
  5. Enjoy!
