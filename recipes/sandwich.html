Turkey Sandwich

Description

We love sandwiches. Learn how to make a turkey cheese sandwich here.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slice Bread
  • Mayo
  • Deli cheese of choice
  • Deli turkey slices
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Grab the bread slices and set them down flat.
  2. Spread mayo on both slices evenly.
  3. Place cheese on one slice.
  4. Place meat on top of cheese.
  5. Put lettuce and tomato on top of that.
  6. Place remaining bread slice on top to complete.
  7. Enjoy!
